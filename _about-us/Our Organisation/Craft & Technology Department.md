### Department Goals:

*   To promote the teaching and learning of Art, Design & Technology (D&T) and Music curriculum    
*   To provide Students with an enjoyable and positive experience in learning about Art, D&T and Music     
*   To identify Students' talents and strengths in Art, D&T and Music and further develop their potential  
    

### Department Objectives:

*   To stimulate and sustain Students' interest in Art, D&T and Music curriculum    
*   To set high expectations for Art, D&T and Music  
*   To develop students' talents using pupil-centered approach      
*   To maximise the use of IT in teaching  
*   To organize Enrichment Programmes for the Students
*   To infuse thinking skills and creativity in the learning of Art, D&T and Music

  

### Department Programmes:

##### Interdisciplinary Project Work (IPW): Sec 1

This is a collaboration between the Art, D&T and Music Unit of the C&T Department.

**Learning Objectives:**

*   D&T: Students learn and apply teamwork and workshop skills to create a D&T Cajon prototype out of plywood
*   Music: Students learn about rhythms, formations, cues and signals to play a piece of music using the hand-made Cajon
*   Art: Students appreciate aesthetics and express creativity (Art) through designing a design skin/decal for their handmade Cajon
*   Students see the Inter-connectedness of how they can apply the knowledge and skills learnt across Art, D&T and Music curriculum
