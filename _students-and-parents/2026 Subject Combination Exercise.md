<h2>Secondary 2 Subject Combination Exercise 2026</h2>
<p>Choosing a Upper Secondary subject combination is an important milestone
in every Springfielder's learning journey. The Subject Combination Exercise
helps our Secondary 2 students make informed decisions about the subjects
they will study in Upper Secondary, based on their <strong>interests</strong>, <strong>strengths</strong>, <strong>aspirations</strong>,
and <strong>academic readiness</strong>.</p>
<p>Through the Subject Combination Exercise, students will learn more about
the various subject offerings, understand the eligibility requirements
for different combinations, and consider how their choices may relate to
their future educational and career pathways.</p>
<p></p>
<h3><u>Briefing for Parents / 29 May 2026</u></h3>
<p>Parents play a key role in guiding and supporting their child through
the Subject Combination Exercise. <strong>We encourage parents to engage their child in thoughtful conversations about their interests, strengths, and aspirations.</strong>
</p>
<p>These discussions can help students gain a deeper understanding of themselves
and make informed decisions about the subjects and pathways that best support
their growth and future goals.</p>
