<h1><strong>KeepSGClean Award 2026 – Gold Tier</strong></h1>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/cleansg1.jpg">
</div>
<p>Springfield Secondary School is proud to be a recipient of the KeepSGClean
Award 2026 (CleanHood), achieving the Gold Tier in our very first submission.
This national recognition reflects the collective effort of our students
and staff in keeping our school environment clean, green, and welcoming
for all. Guided by the values of shared responsibility and graciousness
championed by the Keep Singapore Clean Movement, our school community continues
to play an active role in caring for our environment.</p>
<p>This achievement is a reminder that every small action counts. Together,
we strive to build a cleaner, kinder, and more sustainable future.</p>
