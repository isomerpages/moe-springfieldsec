<h1>Springfield Explorer</h1>
<p></p>
<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>The Springfield Explorer is Springfield Secondary School’s unique adaptation
of the Ministry of Education’s Student-Initiated Learning (SIL) programme.</p>
<p>Guided by our vision to develop Future-Ready Learners with a Caring Heart
and the Confidence to Lead. Springfield Explorer empowers students to take
ownership of their learning.</p>
<p>Rooted in our STRIDE values (Service, Tenacity, Respect, Integrity, Diligence,
Empathy) the programme encourages students to cultivate curiosity, engage
in self-discovery, and apply their skills in authentic, meaningful ways.</p>
<p></p>
<h3>Overview</h3>
<p><em>Focus Areas:</em>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Student Agency</strong> – encourage students to initiate and lead
their own learning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Lifelong Learning</strong> – nurture intrinsic motivation and a
passion for discovery.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Holistic Development</strong> – support growth across four domains:
Heart, Body, Mind, and Spirit.</p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Student Outcomes:</em><strong><br></strong>Through Springfield Explorer,
students will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate self-discipline and initiative in pursuing personal learning
goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Apply learning to benefit their community and environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reflect on their growth, aligning actions and attitudes with STRIDE values.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>Key Programmes &amp; Initiative </h3>
<h4>Four Learning Domains</h4>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Heart – Service to the Community<br></strong> Fosters compassion,
empathy, and active citizenship.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>&nbsp;<em>Examples:</em>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Food Bank Singapore</strong> – assist in food sorting, packing,
or volunteering in outreach and events.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>HCA Hospice Care</strong> – befriend seniors, engage in creative
activities, accompany them to appointments.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Voices for Animals</strong> – promote adoptions, foster rescues,
or help with operations.</p>
<p></p>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>

discipline, and healthy habits.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ol>

</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Master the “7 Headstand” for strength and balance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn trending dances like the “Slickback” to improve coordination.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Practice football juggling to build agility and focus.
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight">

<p><strong>Mind – Knowledge and Skill</strong>
<br>Enhances cognitive abilities, creativity, and problem-solving skills.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<p><em>Examples:</em>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Play chess for strategic thinking and concentration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create games with Scratch to learn coding concepts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn a new language via Busuu to appreciate other cultures.
<br>
</p>
