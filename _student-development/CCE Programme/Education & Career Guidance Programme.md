<h1><strong>Eduational &amp; Career Guidance Programme</strong></h1>
<p>ECG Coordinator: Ms Zhang Lisa
<br>Email:&nbsp;<a href="mailto:zhang_lisa@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">zhang_lisa@moe.edu.sg</a>
<br>Office No.: 6318 3053</p>
<p>The Education and Career Guidance (ECG) programme aims to equip Springfielders
with the knowledge, skills and attitudes that will enable them to cope
with change, adapt and make informed education and career choices. This
is achieved through an integration of Career Education lessons, Study Skills
techniques, Learning Journeys to tertiary institutions and industries,
School Careers Fair and a pilot Work Experience Programme.</p>
<p>To discuss any of the following, please book appointment slots that <strong>do NOT conflict with lessons and CCA.</strong>
<br>• Raising self-awareness (VIPS, RIASEC, etc.)
<br>• Subject Combination (e.g. Sec 2 / JC)
<br>• Post-Secondary options / pathways
<br>• Preparation for different Admission Exercises, e.g. JIE, JAE, DSA-JC,
EAE, etc.
<br>• Factors to consider in ECG planning
<br>• Any other ECG related Questions</p>
