<p>
<br>
<br>1. Springfield Secondary School's Football team has been around since
1994 and is committed to inculcating the love for football, discipline
in training and preparing our boys for friendly matches and tournaments.
<br>
<br>2. Students will be developed physically and mentally regardless of the
talent and abilities they already have. It will definitely test their endurance
and resilience as the activities will be very physically in nature.
<br>
<br>3. The Springfield Football team welcomes any students with the talent,
discipline, love of football and the ability to commit to training sessions
and matches.
<br>
<br>
<br>4. Students will get to represent the school in regional/national level.
They will also get the opportunity to play with various established clubs
from Singapore as well as friendly matches with students from international
schools.</p>
