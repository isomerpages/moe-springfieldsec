<h4>Our Mission</h4>
<p>Growing Through Music Making, Leading with Service, Integrity, and Humility</p>
<h4>Our Vision</h4>
<p>To inspire confident, compassionate musicians who lead with integrity
and a love for music.</p>
<h4>Objectives</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Instrumental Playing:</strong> Equip students with technical playing
skills to achieve proficiency in their chosen instruments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Music Knowledge:</strong> Provide a strong foundation in music
theory to enhance both performance and creativity.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Collaboration and Discipline:</strong> Cultivate teamwork and a
disciplined approach to music making, fostering an inclusive and cohesive
band culture.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Leadership Development:</strong> Develop leadership skills by providing
students with opportunities to take on responsibilities and peer mentoring.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4>Outcomes</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students will achieve musical proficiency and confidence in both individual
and ensemble performances.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The band will develop future leaders, enhancing students’ organizational
and communication skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The band will contribute to school and community events, developing talents
and promoting the arts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Graduates will become lifelong learners with a passion for music and have
cherished memories of their band experience.</p>
</li>
</ol>
