### Start-IT-Right (SIR) Programme 

<b>Secondary 1 to 3: 2 January to 7 January 2025<br>
Secondary 4 to 5:  2 January to 10 January 2025</b>



The SIR is a unique programme designed and customised to provide the students with a holistic and purposeful embarkation for the new academic year. It will cover major components of academic learning, information technology engagement, positive classroom culture and personal, emotional &amp; social well-being. The students will undergo a series of hands-on, team-based and interactive activities as they learn to build positive bonds, develop good habits &amp; routines and strengthen their personal competencies. The students will work closely with the Class Form Teachers to forge a class identity, set daily norms and establish positive peer relationships. For the Sec 1 cohort, the outdoor learning activities will also be incorporated in the SIR to support the students moving into a new learning environment.
